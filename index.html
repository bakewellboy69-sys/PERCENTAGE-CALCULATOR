What Is a Percentage?

A percentage represents a number as a fraction of 100. It is commonly used in finance, statistics, shopping discounts, tax calculations and more.

How To Calculate Percentage Increase

Subtract the original number from the new number, divide by the original, then multiply by 100.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate 20% of a number?

Multiply the number by 0.20.

How do I calculate percentage change?

Use the formula: (New − Original) ÷ Original × 100.
